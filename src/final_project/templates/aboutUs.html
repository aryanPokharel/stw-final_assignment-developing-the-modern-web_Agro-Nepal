AGRO NEPAL

Agro Nepal is a national service corporation, which believes in collectivism to solve national problems.

The main goal of Agro Nepal is to uplift the condition of the Nepalese Agriculture. To do this, we have been travelling to rural parts of Nepal, where majority of Nepalese farmers reside, to teach them about modern ways of agriculture and the importance of agriculture in Nepal.
